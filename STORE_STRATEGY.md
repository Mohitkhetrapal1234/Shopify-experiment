# Store Strategy

## Store concept

Build a premium ethnic Indian womenswear storefront with strong editorial merchandising and a polished wedding-season shopping experience.

## Core catalog

- Sarees
- Lehengas
- Anarkalis
- Kurta sets
- Occasion gowns
- Dupattas and accessories

## Homepage sections

- Cinematic hero with wedding and festive focus
- Occasion-led collection grid
- Featured craftsmanship story
- Bestsellers or new arrivals carousel
- Testimonial or press strip
- Bridal consultation or styling CTA

## Product page priorities

- Fabric, embroidery, lining, and care details
- Size guide and fit notes
- Delivery timeline for made-to-order pieces
- Styling recommendations
- Close-up detail gallery

## Premium signals

- Strong visual hierarchy
- Consistent product photography
- Fabric and craft storytelling
- Simple navigation with high-intent landing pages
